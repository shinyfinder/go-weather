GWS is a simple webtool used to output the current forecast in 150 cities from around the world (max 10 output), updated every hour! The tool is geared toward Pokémon GO players who would like a quick way to find a location with a specific weather. Usage is simple: choose a weather condition and click the button to get the coorindinates of the weather.
